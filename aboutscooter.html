Scott "Scooter" Caudle is a musician, editor, and designer
from Charlotte, North Carolina

I've grown up around music. from my humble beginnings as a toddler asking my mom for piano lessons to starting middle school and picking up a guitar, music has always had a spot in my life. I have always had a do-it-yourself attitude and in 2013 I started a guitar pedal company called Mint Condition Pedals. With the help of my father manning the drill press, he drilled all of the enclosures and I wired everything accordingly. I take great pride in creating utility pedals that help the average guitarist navigate their pedal board a little easier, while not breaking the bank.

Graduating from Gaston Christian School in 2014, I enjoyed 4 years of playing guitar for the praise band and loved learning from the other musicians that I had the pleasure of playing with. With my experiences as a musician, I have grown to love the creative process that comes along with that. Whether its writing songs or setting up guitars, I always strive to my very best at anything that is thrown my way.

Other interests besides music include editing media in different programs including After Effects, GIMP, and Photoshop. I also am learning front end development, so I've gotten my feet wet in HTML, CSS and a little JavaScript.
I have a passion for working with other people on musical projects and other creative ideas and I'd love to work with you!

oh, and call me scooter. ☺
